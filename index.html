Our Story

Moments Frozen
in Light

Studio K is a boutique photography and film studio based in Chennai, dedicated to capturing the poetry in every human moment. From sacred wedding rituals to tender family portraits and polished commercial work, we approach every frame with the same reverence.

Founded by Babu N, the studio has earned a reputation for its cinematic sensibility, obsessive attention to light, and the ability to put subjects completely at ease — letting genuine emotion surface naturally.
